  • Name: Porson, Richard
  • Gender: Male
  • Birth Date: 25  December  1759
  • Death Date: 25  September  1808
  • Occupation: classical scholar

Richard Porson was a famous classical scholar, originally from Norfolk. He formed part of Godwin's early circle, often dining with him and Thomas Holcroft. He appears in Hazlitt's The life of Thomas Holcroft. Godwin's entry for his death-date is consistent with the DNB entry.
