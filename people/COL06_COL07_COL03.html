  • Name: Collier, John Payne
  • Gender: Male
  • Birth Date: 11  January  1789
  • Death Date: 17  September  1883
  • Occupation: literary editor
  • Occupation: forger

All instances of J/Jno/JP Collier/Collier junr are coded as John Payne Collier as are instances of Collier after 1816 when Godwin's contact with Collier senior declines. All other Colliers are coded as John Dyer Collier (father). 'Colliers' and '2 Colliers' have been coded as John Dyer and Jane Collier except when recorded at Coleridge's lectures where it is taken to be father and son (Jane is not presumed to be there as we would expect '3 Colliers' but this is uncertain). John Payne has been included in all instances of multiple Colliers (e.g. 3 Colliers).

R, M, and W Collier are related to this Collier family (siblings of JP?), appearing as they do with the identified Colliers and with Coleridge amongst others.
