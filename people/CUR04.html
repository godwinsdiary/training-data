  • Birth Date: 1789
  • Death Date: 1858
  • Occupation: writer

William Henry Curran was the son of John Philpot Curran and the brother of Amelia. He wrote a biography of his father which was published in 1819 and also wrote Sketches of the Irish Bar (1855).
