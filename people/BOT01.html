Unidentified. Botwright only appears between 1813 and 1823, though Godwin writes him one more letter in 1826. He first appears through James Kenney, but it is difficult to establish much more through context as he is often alone (mostly calling on Godwin or having tea with him). His peak years of contact are 1817-20. Godwin sees him at Hazlitt's lectures in 1818, also quite often in conjunction with the Kenneys.
