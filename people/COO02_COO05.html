Cooper was the sister of Thomas Cooper (who was a cousin and pupil of Godwin's who went on to be an acclaimed actor). There are entries to Miss Cooper, Miss E Cooper, Miss E P Cooper, EPC, E P Cooper, E Cooper and so on, but we have assumed these are variants for a single person. Many of the contacts are associated with her mother Grace Mary Cooper

Godwin also records her death and funeral.
