Macmillan was one of Godwin's printers. The fourth edition of Godwin's book The Pantheon: or Ancient History of The Gods of Greece and Rome (1814) was printed by B. Macmillan of Bow Street, Covent Garden. However, there are no printers called Macmillan on The British Book Trade Index that match the dates and hence no further information available.

One instance of Macmillan occurs in 1800 which precedes the majority of the contacts by five years; thus, it may not be the same person.
