  • Name: Bonnycastle, John
  • Gender: Male
  • Death Date: 15  May  1821
  • Occupation: mathematician

Bonnycastle was a maths tutor, later professor, and frequent author of maths textbooks. He was a great friend of Henry Fuseli and Godwin knew him through Joseph Johnson's gatherings. He was the father of Charles Bonnycastle, the first professor of natural philosophy at the University of Virginia at its opening in 1825.

Godwin recorded his death.
